Our Great Landforms

Explore Our Landforms

From Stone Mountain to the Chattahoochee River, The Piedmont Region has a great many landforms for you to visit. Hover on an Image to see more.

Stone Mountain

the largest single granite formation in the world this mountain has a nice, long uphill hike with great views and rewards at the top, when it's time to go down, there is a gondola that can take you down to the base, where thare is an abundace of many activities for you.

Arabia Mountain

Arabia Mountain, another rock outcropping, has lots of beautiful plants and views, as well as many amazing animals. They have a large lake and a nature center. And if you are lucky enough, you might even find a geocache!

Panola Mountain

Similarly to Arabia Mountain, Panola Mountain has a lake, a nature center, and is a large rock outcropping. It is also right next to Arabia Mountain.

Chattahoochee River

One of the largest rivers in Georgia, the Chattahoochee River has many trails, a natrue center, and more. It is also a nice place to swim in on the sides, where the water is calm.
