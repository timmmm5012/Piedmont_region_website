Our Abundant Transportation

Explore Our Transportation

The Piedmont Region has many modes of transportation, one of which is the ATL airport, the busiest and one of the most iconic airports.

Roads

The Piedmont Region has many roads, highways, and other vehicle paths. All of these spread all over the Piedmont Region helping you get from point to point very quickly. They also help bridge other modes of transportation together, such as the airport and the railways.

The Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport

100+ Million PPY

The Atlanta airport has served over 100 million passengers per year since 2015 (excluding COVID), so it has a very massive capacity.

7 Terminals

With over 7 different terminals to keep flights separated, and a strain between them all, you can find out where you flight is and get to it quickly with ease.

Delta's HQ

This is the airport where the major airline Delta is headquartered, and because of that, not only is ATL the busiest airport, but it also has over 100 locations served from there.

Many Job Opportunities

Being the world's busiest airport, and with so many airlines and destinations, ATL has a very large number of jobs of many kinds.
